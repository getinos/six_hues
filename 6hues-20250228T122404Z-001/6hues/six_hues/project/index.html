About 6Hues

6Hues Architecture Studio has a truly unique origin story that began 12 years ago with the founder, Aayush Arya. Back when he was still in 11th grade, Aayush had a passion for technology journalism, which led him to spend seven years deeply immersed in that field. However, his journey took a fascinating turn when he discovered his love for interior design. By 2012, Aayush decided to change his career and founded 6Hues Architecture Studio. The journey of 6Hues Architecture Studio toward perfection has been a thrilling and ongoing adventure, and we're far from finished. Our commitment to excellence drives us to continually refine and enhance the experiences we offer our clients.

With a passion for design excellence, we blend creativity with practicality to bring ideas to life. From residential projects to large-scale commercial developments, 6Hues is committed to shaping the future of architecture.
